Geospatial Topic Modeling of Restaurants Using the Yelp Academic Dataset

  • 4986 Active Restaurants

  • 166825 Reviews of Active Restaurants

  • ~ 6901.6 Square Miles

In 2002 David Blei, Andrew Ng, and Michael Jordan came up with the LDA (Latent Dirichlet Allocatin) algorithm to discover topics within documents. LDA is today the most commononly used topic modeling algorithm. LDA has been used from finding subtopics amongst papers to processing images. I plan on using LDA to geopraphically describe the city and surrounding area of Scotsdale, Arizona. Feel free to look around the site and stay tuned for updates!
